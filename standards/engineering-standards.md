# Engineering Standards

## Purpose

These standards provide shared engineering conventions for the project. Individual applications may extend these with app-specific standards in their own repositories, but project-wide expectations are recorded here.

> Fill in sections as technology choices and conventions are confirmed. Do not invent standards prematurely.

## General Principles

- **Consistency over cleverness.** Prefer standard patterns and clear code over novel approaches.
- **Fail explicitly.** Errors should be visible, logged, and actionable.
- **Design for change.** Applications will evolve; prefer loose coupling and clear interfaces.
- **Document boundaries.** Where applications interact, the contract must be explicit and versioned.
