## Problem

1. The `Chci early access` CTA button does not currently navigate to the contact page. It should.
2. The pricing section(s) describe the trial or offer in a way that does not reflect the current offering. The product owner wants to communicate a **one-week free trial** in all pricing sections.

## Desired Outcome

- Every instance of the `Chci early access` button (on homepage, pricing page, or wherever it appears) links to the contact page (`/kontakt` for Czech, `/contact` or equivalent for English).
- All pricing sections are updated to reflect a one-week free trial offer. Existing pricing copy that implies a different trial period or offer structure is updated.

## Constraints

- The CTA button link must use the correct localized path (e.g., `/${lang}/kontakt`), not a hardcoded string.
- Both Czech and English pricing copy must be updated.
- Do not change the visual design of the pricing cards — copy update only.

## Suspected Affected Areas

- `src/components/sections/Hero.tsx` or equivalent — likely contains the CTA button
- `src/app/[lang]/cenik/` — pricing page
- `src/components/sections/Pricing.tsx` or equivalent pricing section component
- `src/dictionaries/cs.json` and `en.json` — CTA button label and pricing copy
- `src/lib/constants.ts` — may contain the CTA href if it is a shared constant

## Confirmed Facts

- `Chci early access` CTA button must link to the contact page (from requirement pack).
- Pricing sections should show a one-week free trial (from requirement pack).
- The site has a pricing page at `/cenik` (confirmed: `app-3-overview.md` directory listing).

## Assumptions

- Inferred: The `Chci early access` button currently links to something else (possibly `/demo`, the early access form on homepage, or an external URL). The current target must be confirmed by reading the component.
- Inferred: The pricing page at `src/app/[lang]/cenik/` renders a pricing section component or has inline pricing content.
