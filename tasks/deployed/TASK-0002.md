## Problem

1. The contact page currently shows outdated or missing contact details. The product owner has specified the correct email and phone numbers to display.
2. The contact form includes a `typ praxe` (practice type) field that should be removed. Collecting this information is no longer desired at this stage.

## Desired Outcome

- Contact page displays:
  - Email: `anote-appka@outlook.com`
  - Phone 1: `+420 739 168 738`
  - Phone 2: `+420 735 852 353`
- The `typ praxe` field is removed from the contact form UI.
- The `typ praxe` field is removed from the form's Zod validation schema and API route handler (so the field is no longer validated or stored).
- Submissions saved to `data/submissions.json` and sent via email no longer include `typ praxe`.

## Constraints

- Both Czech and English versions of the contact page must reflect the changes (dict files and/or component).
- The contact form API route (`src/app/api/contact/route.ts`) uses Zod for server-side validation — the schema must be updated to match the new form shape.
- Do not break existing form submissions already stored in `data/submissions.json` — the field removal is additive (old records may have the field, new ones will not).

## Suspected Affected Areas

- `src/app/[lang]/kontakt/` — contact page component and layout
- `src/dictionaries/cs.json` and `en.json` — contact info strings and form field labels
- `src/app/api/contact/route.ts` — Zod schema and email template
- `src/components/sections/` or contact-specific components — if contact info is rendered in a section component
