# Task: Append mandatory safety disclaimer beneath dose modification recommendations in Diabetický poradce 2

## Problem

The "Diabetický poradce 2" advisor (`Id: diabetes-2`) in the medical_advisor Blazor Server app
is the only advisor permitted to give insulin dose suggestions and other diabetes treatment
dose modification recommendations (per `app-1-overview.md` and `DIABETES2_TECH_SPEC.md`). Today,
when this advisor produces a dose modification recommendation, there is no guaranteed Czech-language
safety disclaimer rendered beneath that recommendation that:

1. States the tool is educational and not a certified medical device.
2. Reminds the user that the advisor lacks detailed personal information.
3. Recommends consulting a physician.
4. Warns about hypoglycemia risk from over-dosing insulin or other diabetes medication.

Given the patient-safety and regulatory framing of dose recommendations (non-certified,
hypoglycemia risk), the absence of a guaranteed disclaimer is a safety gap.

## Desired Outcome

Whenever Diabetický poradce 2 outputs a dose modification recommendation (insulin or other
diabetes treatment dose change), the following exact Czech disclaimer text appears beneath
that recommendation, verbatim:

> Důležité upozornění: Toto je pouze edukační nástroj a není certifikovaným zdravotnickým
> prostředkem. Tento poradce, na rozdíl o vašeho lékaře, o vás nemá detailní informace
> a výpočet dávky inzulin, výběr typu inzulinu nebo jiných přípravků k léčbě diabetu
> a jejich dávkování je proto pouze orientační. Doporučujeme proto vždy konzultaci lékaře.
> Při aplikaci vyšší než potřebné dávky inzulinu či jiných přípravků k léčbě diabetu hrozí
> hypoglykemie.

Constraints on the outcome:

- Disclaimer applies **only** to Diabetický poradce 2 (`diabetes-2`), not to the other 4 advisors.
- Disclaimer applies **only** beneath replies that contain a dose modification recommendation,
  not on every reply.
- The disclaimer text must be reproduced verbatim (including the existing minor Czech phrasing
  "na rozdíl o vašeho lékaře" — not corrected, per the original request).

## Constraints

- **Patient-safety relevant.** Any change to dose-recommendation behavior must preserve the
  existing non-certification warnings already documented in `DIABETES2_TECH_SPEC.md`.
- **Czech-language correctness.** Verbatim reproduction is required; do not translate or
  paraphrase.
- **No regression** to the other 4 advisors (`diabetes`, `gestational-diabetes`, `insulin-pump`,
  `insulin-pump-2`).
- **Streaming compatible.** Output flows through Semantic Kernel streaming and the
  `[QUICK_REPLIES]...[/QUICK_REPLIES]` parser in `MedicalAdvisorService`. Whatever mechanism
  is chosen must not break that parser, the quick-reply rendering, or the chat UI streaming.
- **Multilingual behavior.** Disclaimer is always Czech regardless of user language (regulatory
  requirement — confirmed by implementation).

## Implementation

> Status: **Implemented & tested locally** as of 2026-04-30. Awaiting manual smoke test and

`diabetes-2` advisor gate ensures no other advisor is affected. The verbatim Czech phrasing
is preserved exactly as specified (including "na rozdíl o vašeho lékaře" — not corrected).
