# Session Handoff: ANOTE Marketing Brief

## Session Date

2026-04-24

## Goal

Create a new English markdown document describing the ANOTE application for future marketing use, based on direct analysis of `ANOTE_mobile` and `ANOTE-web` plus previously used outreach materials.

## What Was Learned

- ANOTE's strongest marketing story is the reduction of documentation burden for doctors by converting spoken visits into structured medical reports.
- The clearest confirmed differentiators are: structured output instead of plain transcription, Czech-focused medical workflows, multiple examination/report types, local and cloud transcription options, mobile-first use, and a live browser demo.
- Privacy and GDPR-oriented messaging are strong but must stay precise. Safe phrasing centers on local mode, audio staying on-device in local mode, EU-focused processing, and privacy-first design. Broad compliance or certification claims should be avoided.
- Existing website copy already contains many useful external-facing messages, but a shared source brief was missing for reuse across campaigns, outreach, and content production.
