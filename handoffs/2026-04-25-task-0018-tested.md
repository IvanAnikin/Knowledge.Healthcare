## What Was Learned

- The duplicate pricing-page title affected both `/cenik` and `/en/pricing`, not only the Czech route.
- Root cause was stacked headings from two sources: the route-level `PageHeader` and an internal heading block inside `Pricing.tsx`.
- `PageHeader` rendered `dict.pricing.heading` plus `dict.pricingPage.subtitle`, while `Pricing.tsx` separately rendered `dict.pricing.heading` plus `dict.pricing.subheading`.
- Removing the inner heading block from `Pricing.tsx` resolved the duplicate-title issue without leaving broken spacing.
- Reported validation passed with both `npx tsc --noEmit` and `npm run build`.
